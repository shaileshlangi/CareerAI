# **App Name**: CareerAI

## Core Features:

- AI Resume Optimization: Paste a job description, and our AI tool suggests resume edits optimized for applicant tracking systems (ATS).
- Job Board: A directory listing of available job positions, filterable by industry and location.
- Company Profiles: Allow companies to post detailed profiles.
- Job Application Tracking: Enable job seekers to track the status of their applications.
- User Authentication: Secure user authentication with role-based access control.
- Pricing Plans Display: Show a comparison of features in the Job Seeker and Employer pricing plans.
- Homepage Sections: Display content sections including a Hero Banner, Features, How It Works, Testimonials, Pricing, and CTAs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep periwinkle (#5469D4) to convey stability, trust, and innovation in the job search and recruitment process.
- Background color: Very light periwinkle (#F0F2FA) to provide a calm and professional backdrop, allowing content to stand out.
- Accent color: Electric violet (#A259FF) for highlights, interactive elements, and call-to-action buttons, ensuring they grab attention.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a modern, humanist sans-serif, ensures readability and clarity across all devices.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', an elegant, high-contrast sans-serif, conveys a high-end and fashionable feel, capturing the target audience's attention and emphasizing important messaging.
- Incorporate glassmorphism effects on key UI elements for a trendy, modern look. Apply pastel gradients to backgrounds for a Gen-Z/Millennial friendly design.
- Use subtle, modern animations on the homepage to give it a professional feel.
